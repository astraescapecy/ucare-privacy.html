This Privacy Policy explains how UCare (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the UCare iOS app.

1. Summary

UCare is a wellness and habit-design app. In the current release, most data stays on your iPhone. We do not operate a UCare account server in this version. Optional features may send limited data to third parties as described below.

2. Information We Collect

  • Profile and program data: name, email, username, care goals, questionnaire answers, generated protocol steps, step completions, weekly check-ins, Glow-Up Score inputs, and reminder preferences — stored locally on your device.
  • Photos (optional): face or skin photos you choose during onboarding skin analysis, profile avatar, and weekly progress photos — stored as files on your device unless you delete them in the app.
  • Apple Health (optional): if you enable blending, UCare may read dietary water and sleep samples from HealthKit on this device to adjust your score. UCare does not upload Health data to our servers.
  • Notifications: local reminder schedules you configure (water, morning routine, skincare before bedtime, trial-end reminder). No push marketing.
  • Subscription status: handled by Apple (StoreKit). We store entitlement flags locally on your device.
  • AI protocol generation (optional, subscribers): when enabled, a summary of your goals and questionnaire answers may be sent to OpenAI to generate personalized protocol text. Photos are not sent for this feature in the current app version.

3. On-Device Skin Analysis

Skin score shown during onboarding is computed on your device from the photo you provide. It is a wellness estimate, not medical advice or a diagnosis.

4. How We Use Information

  • Build and display your daily protocol and progress
  • Schedule local reminders you opt into
  • Verify UCare Plus subscriptions through Apple
  • Optionally personalize protocol copy via OpenAI when that feature is active
  • Respond to support requests you send us

5. What We Do Not Do (this release)

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not show third-party ads.
  • We do not upload your full HealthKit history to UCare servers.

6. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with service providers needed to run the app:

  • Apple — App Store billing, subscription management, and system permissions
  • OpenAI — optional text generation for subscribed users when the feature is configured

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

7. Storage, Retention, and Security

Data is stored in the app sandbox (UserDefaults and Application Support). You can export JSON from Profile, delete progress photos, remove your avatar, log out, or delete your local account from Profile. Log out and delete do not cancel App Store subscriptions — use Apple’s subscription settings.

8. Your Choices

  • Revoke photo, notification, or Health access in iOS Settings
  • Turn off Health blending in Profile
  • Export or delete on-device data from Profile
  • Contact us for privacy questions or deletion help: support@ucare.app

9. Children

UCare is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country).

10. International Processing

If you use optional cloud features (such as OpenAI), data may be processed outside your country by those providers under their policies.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. We will revise the effective date above when we do.
